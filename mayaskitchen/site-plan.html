Overview

Purpose

Who here loves baked goods? Baked goods that they don’t have to make? So delicious and so easy! I will happily bake them for you at my business Maya’s Kitchen, and either have them ready for pick up or delivery. All you have to do is message what you want, and I work with you to get your dream in a great smelling dessert! After that you pay and boom, delicious goodness in your home. Message me today!

Audience

To those who love baked goods, but don't have time to do it themselves, or just don't like baking! I am here for you!
